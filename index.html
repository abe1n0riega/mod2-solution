Chicken

Chicken is tender, delicious, rich in nutritional value, and has nourishing effects on health. Chicken dishes are available all over China, and it is the most common white meat. Chinese people believe that nearly all parts of a chicken can be eaten, including the feet.

Beef

Black pepper beef is a famous Cantonese dish. This dish is made from tender pieces of beef fried with black pepper, onions, and green and red bell peppers. The beef tastes smooth and delicious; the onion and red and green bell peppers are crispy. Learn more about how to cook black pepper beef.

Sushi

Traditional sushi is served with cut rolls wrapped in seaweed or soy paper. Creative additions like cream cheese, spicy mayo, and fried buns reflect a distinct Western influence that sushi connoisseurs alternately love and despise. Even vegetarians can enjoy modern vegetable-style sushi rolls.
